TCHOU VERSION 2
by Debray Matthieu

Tchou is a green ET with big ears. 
He wins a ship at the lottery and now explores the universe. 
But today, he enters the galaxy of the Orange Evil Octopus? 
The goal is to survive many waves of foe, each wave coming with is own trajectory, resistance and speed. 
After some waves (depending of the level you choose) you'll reach the Boss . 

Try to survive to him now.
