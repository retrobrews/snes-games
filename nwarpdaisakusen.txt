N-Warp Daisakusen v1.0
By d4s

N-Warp Daisakusen is a deathmatch minigame for eight human players. 
Connect two Multitaps and eight joypads to your SNES, invite some friends and compete for the first place by beating and kicking the shit out of the other players. 
This game is available for download completely free of charge.
